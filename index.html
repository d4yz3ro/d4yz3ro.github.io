Hello:) I am an incoming Ph.D. student at HKUST, advised by Prof. Yike Guo. During my time as a Research Assistant in PAIR Lab at Peking University, I was advised by Prof. Yaodong Yang, where I explored methods to mitigate deceptive alignment. I also contributed to the Align-Anything framework for post-training and value alignment. I am passionate about building secure and reliable AI systems, focusing on Reinforcement Learning, AI Alignment & Safety.
